Level Design for 'Project Diablo 2' - a community based 'mod' of the cult classic ARPG, Diablo 2. Creation of these environments was acheieved through the use of a variety of bespoke software packages, utilising data taken from Diablo 2's source material. Thematically, the levels are loosely based on Diablo's extensive 'lore', combined with my own interepretation of a number of creative themes.
